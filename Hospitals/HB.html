Causes of low Hb include: Iron deficiency anemia, Vitamin deficiency anemia (B12/Folic acid), Aplastic anemia, Anemias associated with bone marrow disease, Hemolytic anemia etc.
Causes of high Hb include: Smoking, COPD (Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease) exacerbation, Dehydration, Emphysema, Heart failure, Polycythemia Vera.
